Project: CENO
Year: 2016
Category: Branding & Web Design

CENO is a NYC based studio with a unifying respect for the culture of the youth, and a desire to listen, not dictate. We were conceived due to our individual experiences with misrepresentation in the media, and quickly noticed that this discomfort extended into a much larger population of artists. We create with a primary consideration toward the message we are putting out, and not our own status -- working in a space where concept and community are equalizing forces. In this way, each one of our projects demands to be visually striking and explicitly unique to who we collaborate with.

  • Members: Asha Maura, Elijah Maura, Naya Samuel, Elias Paulson
